# V2.4 Journal Strategy Record

## First target

Knowledge-Based Systems remains the first target. The paper is scientifically
defensible as a reliability audit of extracted graph knowledge, but submission
risk is high because the contribution is audit-focused and empirically lighter
than method-positive causal-discovery papers.

## Fallbacks

- **Neural Networks:** topical for neural score semantics and attribution, but
  not necessarily easier.
- **Neurocomputing:** plausible technical fallback for the neural-method and
  evaluation framing.
- **Engineering Applications of Artificial Intelligence:** not a strong current
  fit without a genuine engineering application.

Venue strategy must not revive unsupported ISTF performance claims or convert
the boundary study into a positive method result.
