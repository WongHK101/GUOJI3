# V2.4 Critical Scientific Corrections

1. **Controlled architecture made self-contained.** The manuscript now states
   that `c=g_phi(X)` is a learned causal full-prefix transform, identifies the
   encoder, predictor tensor, target domain, detach points, x-only derivatives,
   full-penalty coordinates, and the still-partial graph score.
2. **Graph-object cases separated.** External `c`, a conditional graph over
   `X|c`, a joint graph over `(X,c)`, and transformed input `c=g_phi(X)` are no
   longer conflated.
3. **Jacobian-ratio terminology corrected.** Every v2.4 document calls the
   frozen quantity a mean absolute Jacobian-magnitude ratio. It defines the two
   blockwise means and states that the ratio is scale dependent and not
   invariant to coordinate rescaling or reparameterization.
4. **All full-penalty variants disclosed.** The appendix reports baseline,
   concat x-only, equal-lambda, budget-normalized, `lambda_c/lambda_x=0.1`, and
   `lambda_c/lambda_x=10` using the frozen five-seed means and population SDs.
   The experiment is explicitly exploratory; no optimum or monotonicity is
   claimed.
5. **Single-run abstract disclosure added.** The capacity and coefficient
   numbers are introduced as two single-run controlled diagnostics before any
   value is reported.
6. **Closest-work distinction established.** Official primary references now
   cover attribution axioms, sanity checks, faithfulness evaluation and
   attribution regularization, with a graph-score-specific distinction.

No empirical value, seed, checkpoint, model weight or frozen conclusion was
changed.
