## Citation claim added

Related Work now states that general attribution research supplies axioms,
sanity checks, faithfulness criteria and explanation regularization, while the
present contribution audits a directed graph-score knowledge object. The
claimed distinction is joint score-route/penalty-route treatment, explicit
source-coordinate and temporal-horizon checks, dependence on the declared
conditional/joint/transformed-input graph object, and a profile of coexisting
failures.

No citation is used to imply that the proposed audit is a causal-identifiability
theorem or a replacement for general saliency evaluation.
